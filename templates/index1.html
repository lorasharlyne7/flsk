BLOSSOMS OF THE SAVANNAH

Taiyo and Resian lived with their parents mr and mrs ole kaelo in nakuru
and one day they had to relocate since their papa had been retrenched from the agribix company
.they had to move to their village Nasila where their relatives lived because it was their native land
.A home coming ceremony was held and they were welcomed by their uncles and aunts
.resian realized that one of her uncles wife was pregnant
.They were faced by several challenges like the attack by two vagabonds.

Joseph parmuat who was resian and taiyos cousin was given a responsibility to teach the girls about their tradition
since they had been away since birth so they didnt know about the nasilian culture
their mother was told to prepare the girls for the female genital mutilation in which the girls were against
it and they planned on escaping but Taiyo was not lucky since she was forced
but resian with the help of her nurse was able to go to emakererei
and was later taken to their dream university to persue their dream courses
